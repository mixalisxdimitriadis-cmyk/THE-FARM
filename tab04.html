Silver Outstanding in International Wine and Spirit Competition 2015 (IWSC). ENOSIS is Greek for union. People under the same believe and care, gathered, inspired and created a unique spirit the original Mastiha liqueur. Chios Mastiha, this unique resin gifted with a distinctive flavour and aroma, travelled through time as a special ingredient of the Mediterranean nutrition. Chios Mastiha represents the Grower’s Spirit, a whole theory, that unites faith, labour, know how and originality.
