NEMEA WINERY

Branding and packaging design for a Greek Winery
2025
Creative Direction
Design & Production

The logo name and design were created using the first letters of each word, resulting in the letters NW. However, it was uniquely tailored to fit the specific category of the winery and ultimately featured a cork opener at the end. The endline aimed to capture the essence of wine culture the joy of life. However, designing the labels posed a challenge, as they were intended for various target groups, necessitating changes in style, color, and design elements.
